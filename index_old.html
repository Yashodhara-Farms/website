Our Story: A Journey of Purity

Yashodhara Farms was born from a simple belief: pure food is the foundation of a healthy life. Nestled in the pristine foothills of Uttarakhand, we are a family-run initiative dedicated to reviving the timeless traditions of Vedic agriculture.

For the conscious urban family seeking refuge from a hectic world, we offer more than just organic products; we offer a promise of authenticity. We are inspired by the wisdom of our ancestors and the untouched beauty of the Himalayas, ensuring that every product reaching your home is crafted with care, integrity, and a deep respect for nature.

Why Yashodhara Farms?

In a market full of claims, we stand for trust and transparency. We go beyond labels to bring you food that is truly nourishing.

The A2 Cow Advantage

Our Ghee is exclusively from the milk of Gir cows, cherished in Ayurveda for its nutritious and easily digestible A2 protein.

Commitment to Tradition

We don't take shortcuts. From the slow, manual Bilona churning of Ghee to the patient cold-pressing of oils, we honour the methods that preserve nutrients.

Complete Transparency

We believe you have the right to know what's in your food. We invite your questions and are proud of our process. With us, what you see is what you get.
