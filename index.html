Our Farming Practices

At Yashodhara Organic Farms, our farming philosophy is deeply rooted in traditional wisdom and sustainable methods. We are committed to nurturing the soil and our produce naturally, ensuring chemical-free, nutrient-rich food while protecting the environment and supporting biodiversity.

Organic Cultivation

We avoid synthetic chemicals, focusing instead on natural composting, crop rotation, and eco-friendly pest management to cultivate a variety of cereals, pulses, and oilseeds.

Indigenous Cow-Based Dairy

Our dairy operations exclusively feature indigenous Indian cow breeds. These resilient cows are nurtured with love, fed organic fodder, and milked ethically.

Traditional Bilona Ghee

Using the traditional Bilona method, we produce pure A2 ghee, known for its rich aroma, immunity-boosting properties, and role in Ayurvedic wellness.

By combining these practices, we ensure that every product from Yashodhara Organic Farms is a testament to purity, health, and respect for nature.
